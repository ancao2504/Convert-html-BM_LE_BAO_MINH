2. Party B shall make payment in the following method:

Total payment amount = (Monthly print charge) + (Monthly charge)

3. On a monthly basis, Party B shall make payment to Party A within 45 days as of the date the valid invoice issued by Party A is received. If after the stipulated payment period Party B fails to make payment to Party A, Party B shall be subject to a fine of 0.2% of the overdue amount per day.

Article 6: Conditions for the operating quality of the machine

Party A:

1. Shall ensure the machine is always operating in good condition.

2. Shall provide free-of-charge maintenance, repair, spare parts, toner (except for paper).

Party B:

1. Shall ensure that the machine is operated at the correct capacity as specified by the manufacturer: Maximum 16.000 copies/ month/ machine

2. Shall ensure machine storage conditions such as AC power, machine location, ambient temperature, humidity comply with criteria set out by the manufacturer.

3. Shall operate the machine properly, use standard paper, and prevent any foreign object from falling into the machine.

4. Shall not carry out the repair work arbitrarily nor engage any technician who is not authorized by Party A to do the repair work.

Article 7: Provisions for replacing spare parts and repair costs

Party B:

Shall accept and bear all risks of theft and / or damages or losses to the rented asset, or to any portion of the rented asset arising out of any reason whatsoever in the event of a failure as follows:

a. Problem caused by the user or the machine being repaired by a technician of any parties other than Party A.

b. Unstable power supply, fire, thunder strike, flood, explosion, and other natural disasters.

c. Foreign objects (staples, paper clips, insects, liquids ...) fall into the machine.

Party A:

a. Inspect and quote labor charge and replacement parts at favorable prices.

b. Carry out part replacement with parts supplied by LBM.

Article 8: Ownership of equipment

1. Prohibition against transfer, sale or mortgage:

Party B shall not hold the right to transfer, sell, offer to sell, assign, sublet, put in pledge, mortgage or take any steps that violate Party A’s ownership; and Party B shall not allow any third party to possess or use the machine without Party A’s prior consent in writing.

2. Inspection of the asset for rent:

Party B agrees to give irrevocable permission to Party A, his employee or agent for entering the place where the machine is located for monthly inspecting and checking at any reasonable time; and Party B shall be informed at least one (1) day in advance. Whenever Party A’s agent or its dealer enter the place where the asset is located for inspection, a letter of introduction shall be brought along with that person.

Article 10: Breach/early termination of the rental contract:

Whenever any party fails to comply strictly with one of the terms of this Contract, the other party has the right to unilaterally terminate the contract by giving a written notice to the other party. In such case, Party B shall return the rental asset to Party A within thirty (30) days upon receiving the notice.

Condition of early termination

Party A:
Violates any terms or conditions of the Rental Contract.

1. During the rental term, if a technical problem occurs (not due to user's faults and natural disasters) and Party A cannot overcome;

2. In these cases, Party A shall subject to a payment called Early Termination Fee to Party B, this fee is calculated as follows: (Monthly charge) x (The number of remaining months).

Party B:
Fails to pay the Rental as set out in Article 5 and / or other amounts provided in the Rental Contract within ten (10) days as of the due date;

1. Violates any term or condition of the Rental Contract.

2. Ceases, suspends, liquidates, closes or resolves to close its business, or is likely to enter insolvency.

3. In these cases, Party B shall be subject to a payment called Early Termination Fee to Party A, this fee is calculated as follows: (Monthly charge) x (The number of remaining months).

Article 11: Contract validity

1. The contract takes effect from the date of signing. The charging time starts as soon as Party A hands over the machine to Party B, the parties must make the handover minutes signed by two parties. The minutes is an integral part of the contract.

2. The contract shall be deemed to have been liquidated when two parties have fully fulfilled their obligations under the provisions of the contract without any complaint.

3. Any Party who wishes to amend any term or condition of the Contract must inform the other Party in writing at least 10 days in advance for discussion and proper settlement.

4. In case any dispute relating to this contract arises, the two Parties shall negotiate to resolve first. If the two Parties are unable to agree on a resolution for the dispute, the dispute can be escalated to a competent People’s Court as per the law.

5. This contract is made in 04 copies, each party keeps 02 copies, has the same legal value as from the date of signing. In case of differences between the English and Vietnamese, the Vietnamese version prevails.
